
Robot Chassis Expansion Design – Reference Document

Purpose:
This design modification expands the internal chassis width to accommodate larger components, specifically the Pololu 37D motor (30:1 gear ratio), which requires more horizontal clearance.

Chassis Dimensions:
- Original internal width: 100 mm
- Expanded internal width: 172 mm
- Expansion per side: 36 mm
- Chassis length: 300 mm
- Plate thickness (top and bottom): 2 mm (aluminum)

Bracket System:
- Total brackets: 16 (8 per side)
  - 4 brackets on bottom-left
  - 4 on bottom-right
  - 4 on top-left
  - 4 on top-right
- Bracket material: Stainless steel
- Bracket dimensions:
  - Length: 46 mm
  - Width: 16 mm
  - Thickness: 1.9 mm
  - Hole spacing: 36 mm (center-to-center)
  - Hole size: 5 mm (M5)
- Brackets are mounted flat against the outer sides of the top and bottom plates, facing outward to the side wall flanges.
- Brackets are spaced evenly along the 300 mm chassis length.

Mounting Hardware:
- Screws: M3 machine screws
- Centering washers:
  - Nylon
  - Outer diameter: 5 mm
  - Inner diameter: 2.5 mm (tight fit for M3)
- Top washers:
  - Metal M3 washers
  - Outer diameter: 6–8 mm
  - Thickness: approx. 0.5 mm
- Threadlocker (recommended): Loctite 243 (blue) for vibration resistance

Side Wall Flanges:
- Both left and right side walls have 90° inward flanges.
- Brackets are mounted from plate edge to flange surface to structurally tie the top and bottom plates to the side walls.

Use:
This structure allows the use of longer motors (e.g. Pololu 37D) and improves internal space and mounting flexibility.
